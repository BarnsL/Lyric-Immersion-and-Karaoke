Lyric Immersion and Karaoke runs in your SYSTEM TRAY - it is not a normal window.

WHERE IT IS
   After it starts, look at the bottom-right of your taskbar (the notification
   area, next to the clock). You may need to click the small "^" arrow to show
   hidden icons. You'll see the Lyric Immersion and Karaoke tray icon.

WHERE THE SETTINGS ARE
   RIGHT-CLICK the tray icon to open the full menu. Everything is there - there
   is no separate settings window:

       *  Presets (Gaming / Karaoke)
       *  Opacity, Font size, Position, Display, Scroll style
       *  GPU overlay toggle for the smooth click-through renderer
       *  Sync timing - nudge the lyrics earlier / later
       *  Fast song-change detect (for compilations)
       *  Dancing character on/off
       *  Start with Windows
       *  Wrong lyrics - fix the current song
       *  Quit

   LEFT-CLICK the icon to show / hide the overlay.

HOW TO USE IT
   Just play any song in Spotify, YouTube, or any player, and the lyrics appear
   automatically across your screen - with furigana, romaji, and an English
   translation. The overlay never steals focus, so you can keep working,
   watching, or gaming while it runs.

FIRST RUN
   The very first time a song needs AI lyric generation, the app downloads its
   voice model (about 200 MB) and says so on screen - that happens once. Updates
   are automatic: when a new version ships, the tray menu offers a one-click
   "Install update".
